## How to Play
- **Move** ← → or swipe left/right (mobile)
- **Swing Hammer** → SPACE or **tap** on mobile
- Collect glowing **🌺 roses** for points
- Smash **🪡 thorns** for big bonus
- **Avoid the bees** — they sting!
- P = Pause | M = Music

## ✨ Features
- Progressive difficulty & combo system
- Uplifting background music
- Mobile swipe + tap controls
- Spiky scary club hammer
- Particle effects & squish sounds
- High score saving
